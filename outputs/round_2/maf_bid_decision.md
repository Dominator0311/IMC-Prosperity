# Round-2 MAF bid decision (batch D3)

Hierarchical opponent model + EV-maximising bid for the Round-2
Market Access Fee blind auction.

## Setup

- N teams in cohort: **200** (defensive lower bound; actual cohort is larger).
- MC trials per cell: **10,000**.
- Three opponent priors:
  - **naive-heavy** — 50% bid 0; 30% in [10, 100]; 20% in [200, 2k].
  - **balanced**    — 30% bid 0; 40% in [50, 500]; 25% in [500, 3k]; 5% in [3k, 10k].
  - **aggressive**  — 15% bid 0; 35% in [200, 1.5k]; 35% in [1.5k, 5k]; 15% in [5k, 20k].
- Default mixture weights (subjective): naive-heavy=45%, balanced=40%, aggressive=15%.
- Auction: top-half winners pay their bid; losers pay 0. Ties
  treated as losses (conservative).

## Private value v

Marginal value of full market access (extra +25% order-book volume):

- R1 final scored = +89 970 / 1M ticks. Expected R2 official
  similar magnitude (~+90k).
- PEPPER is pinned at +80 for 99.9% of the run → extra asks past
  the position limit are unconsumed → marginal access value on
  PEPPER is ~+8-12% of PEPPER PnL ≈ +6-10k.
- ASH has no binding limit → marginal access value ~+25% of ASH
  PnL ≈ +2-3k.
- **Central estimate v ≈ 10 000 XIRECs.**
- Sensitivity: also evaluate at v = 6 000 (PEPPER limit binds
  earlier than expected) and v = 15 000 (extra access on PEPPER
  helps more than estimated).

## Final recommendation

**Bid `2300` XIRECs.**

This is the floor-EV-maximising choice under v=10 000 with the
default mixture weights (naive-heavy=45%, balanced=40%, aggressive=15%).
Properties:

- Mixture EV: +7683 (vs the EV-max bid `400`'s +8157; we give up ~474 of upside, ~6%, in exchange for floor protection).
- Worst-case (aggressive opponents) EV: +7588, vs the EV-max bid's worst-case +0.
- Cluster-collapse resilience: bid `2300` > cluster value (1000) → median shift cannot push our bid below it. The EV-max bid 400 collapses
  to ~50% win probability if 30% of teams cluster at 1000 XIRECs.
- Cost ceiling: 2 300 XIRECs is ~2.6 % of expected R2 algo PnL (~+90k).
  Even if v turns out to be at the pessimistic 6k, this bid still has
  positive EV under every prior.

Confidence: **medium**. The opponent priors are subjective. Re-run
this script with adjusted mixture weights to test alternative
consensus assumptions.
